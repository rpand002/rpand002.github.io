Rameswar Panda

I am currently a research scientist at IBM Research AI in Cambridge. At IBM Research, I am interested in solving real world problems using computer vision and machine learning.

I received my Ph.D. from UC Riverside in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Amit K. Roy-Chowdhury. Previously, I obtained my M.S. from Jadavpur University (India) in 2013 supervised by Prof. Ananda S. Chowdhury.

I was very fortunate to have interned at NEC Labs America (Cupertino, Summer 2018), Adobe Research (San Jose, Summer/Fall 2017) and Siemens Corporate Research (Princeton, Summer 2016).

Research

My research interests mainly lie in the areas of computer vision, machine learning and multimedia computing. In particular, my current focus is on learning with limited supervision (transfer learning, few-shot learning) and AutoAI for computer vision problems. During my Ph.D., I worked on video summarization, person re-identification in video surveillance, and multi-modal embedding.
